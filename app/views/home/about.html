Easi Lift is a user-friendly ride sharing web application. It was designed with students in mind, however, it can be used by many different groups of people. It is a carpooling application where users can offer lifts, edit and deletem them, but also book rides and view lift details.

Find a ride will redirect you to the lift available page where you can filter and book lifts. Offer a ride will redirect you to the creating a lift page where you can add your details. Any action will however require a account to verify credentials and link your lifts and bookings. Find a Ride Offer a Ride
